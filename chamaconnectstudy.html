Table Banking Platform: Branding, Website & Interface

ChamaConnect - Website & Interface

iOS, Android, Web & Tablet — 2025

The ChamaConnect table banking solution is developed as a cross-platform solution (web and mobile) to manage financial operations such as contributions, loans, fines, guarantor tracking, income, and expenses.

My Role and Project Experience

As the product designer for ChamaConnect, I shaped its identity and functionality from the ground up. I crafted the brand guidelines and designed the logo to reflect trust and innovation. I authored the product requirements document, defining features like guarantor tracking and expense management to meet user needs. I also designed the website and app interface, ensuring a seamless, intuitive experience across web and mobile platforms.

THE CHALLENGE

Designing for group dynamics posed a unique challenge: the platform needed to cater to multiple users with differing roles (e.g., administrators, contributors) while maintaining clarity and fostering trust. It also had to simplify complex financial interactions for non-technical users.

PRODUCT APPROACH

I approached ChamaConnect by starting with the key idea, defining how to manage contributions and loans. I then built a sitemap to structure the platform, followed by wireframes to layout the user flow. Finally, I crafted prototypes to refine the website and app interface, delivering a user-friendly fintech solution.

.

ChamaConnect Product Journey Summary

ChamaConnect began as a vision to modernize table banking for Kenyan Chamas and SACCOs, addressing issues like fraud and poor record-keeping. The journey started with ideation, mapping out a blockchain-powered solution to manage contributions, loans, and financial transparency. A sitemap laid the groundwork, followed by wireframes to design an intuitive cross-platform interface. Prototypes refined the user experience, integrating smart contracts for automation and blockchain for security. Key features— tamper-proof administration, real-time reporting, and flexible policies—emerged, with M-Pesa integration planned next.

Why We Built ChamaConnect

In Kenya, table banking groups—Chamas and SACCOs—have long been a lifeline for communities, pooling savings and offering loans to fuel dreams.

But too often, these groups faced challenges: lost records, mismanaged funds, and disputes over transparency eroded trust.

That's why ChamaConnect was created—a blockchain-powered solution to bring order and security to this vital tradition.

Designed for Kenyan Chama and SACCO groups, it tackles these pain points head-on with tamper-proof administration for member records, smart contracts to automate contributions and loan approvals, and real-time financial reporting to ensure every shilling is tracked transparently.
